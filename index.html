Features

Built for people who
care about showing up

📅

Smart calendar sync

Connect Google, Apple, or Outlook. Every birthday, anniversary, and special date imports automatically – plus subscription tracking so you always know your monthly spend.

✨
AI-powered

AI wish generator

Generate personalised wishes and cards in seconds. Free users get 2 iterations per occasion. Pro gets unlimited – every message feels genuinely written for that person.

🎁

Curated gift marketplace

Browse hundreds of gifts from independent vendors. Our AI advisor asks about the person and budget, then recommends what they'll actually love. It remembers what you sent last year too.

🔔

Unlimited reminders

Never miss a moment again. Get reminders days, weeks, or months ahead – however you prefer. Free forever, for unlimited occasions and unlimited people.

💝

Occasion memory

Twarro remembers everything – last gifts sent, favourite occasions, and the personal details that make your messages feel genuine and thoughtful every single time.

🌐

Share anywhere

Send wishes and cards via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or any platform. Your loved ones get something beautiful no matter where they are in the world.

🌱

Grow your Seed of Love

Every time you show someone you care, your seed grows a little. Complete 30 meaningful actions and unlock something extraordinary – entirely optional, always meaningful.

01 · Name it
Give your seed a name when you sign up. It's yours to nurture.
02 · Grow it
Send wishes, cards, gifts, and share the app to earn growth actions.
03 · Bloom
Reach 30 actions and your seed becomes a fully grown tree.
🌲 Plant a real tree – we'll send quarterly photo updates
💷 Or take £1 back per completed tree

Completely optional – your choice, always.
