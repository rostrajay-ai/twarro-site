Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 May 2026

Twarro is an AI-powered reminder app that helps people remember birthdays, anniversaries, gifts, subscriptions and meaningful moments. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, reminder details, occasion information, gift preferences, AI prompt inputs, device information and subscription status.

How we use information

  • To provide reminders and notifications
  • To generate personalised wishes and cards
  • To suggest gifts and vendors
  • To process subscriptions
  • To improve Twarro and prevent misuse

AI features

When you use AI features, prompts and generated content may be processed to provide personalised outputs. Please review AI-generated content before sharing it.

Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with hosting providers, analytics providers, payment providers, AI processing partners and legal authorities where required.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your data by contacting hello@twarro.app.
